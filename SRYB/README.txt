The Staff Rating of Youth Behavior or “SRYB” is an assessment tool designed to be used by staff to 
individually rate the behavior of youth ages 5 to 18 in their program on 14 items
 
This R-Project imports and analyses the data collected at OST program sites.

1_SRYB.R : imports, cleans and codes the questions from the short form.
2_SRYB.R : Merges the data with the practitionner ID
